Create new ValuatedRegionNodes inside the current region. These new nodes are automatically added as children to the current node. The parameters are checked to have reasonable values. Only leaf nodes can be split.

Parameters
regionsXNumber of resulting nodes in X direction. At most the resolution in X direction.
regionsYNumber of resulting nodes in Y direction. At most the resolution in Y direction.
regionsZNumber of resulting nodes in Z direction. At most the resolution in Z direction.
